The Enchanted Forest

Deep within the Whispering Woods, a powerful magical crystal has been stolen from the Ancient Oak. The forest's balance has been disturbed, and dark magic is spreading through the realm.

Strange shadows grow between the trees, and whispers of a dark ritual echo through the night. The Blood Moon approaches, and time is running short.

Various magical beings have gathered to solve the mystery and restore harmony to the forest before darkness falls forever.
