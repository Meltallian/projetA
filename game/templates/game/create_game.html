Game Scenario: The Enchanted Forest

Deep within the Whispering Woods, a powerful magical crystal has been stolen from the Ancient Oak. The forest's balance has been disturbed, and dark magic is spreading through the realm. Various magical beings have gathered to solve the mystery and restore harmony to the forest.

Characters

This game will include the following 10 characters:

  1. White Mage - A powerful healer and protector with knowledge of ancient healing arts
  2. Green Mage - A nature-focused mage who can commune with plants and animals of the forest
  3. Magenta Sorcerer - A mysterious spellcaster with unique magenta-colored magic and unknown origins
  4. Forest Fairy - A mischievous and ancient fairy with deep connections to the heart of the forest
  5. Druid - A wise guardian of nature's balance who can shape-shift into various animals
  6. Centaur - A half-human, half-horse being with extensive knowledge of the forest's geography
  7. Wood Nymph - A forest spirit bound to the trees, with the ability to move unseen through the woods
  8. Magic Merchant - A traveling trader of magical items and collector of rare artifacts and stories
  9. Mysterious Wanderer - A cloaked figure who appears and disappears at will, with cryptic knowledge
  10. Elementalist - A powerful magic user who can control the elements of nature - earth, air, fire, and water
This will be revealed at the end of the game

The game will start immediately after creation, and you'll be taken to the game management screen.
